London - Southeast England - United Kingdom

London is the capital and largest city of the UK (United Kingdom) and England, situated in the southeast on the River Thames, serving as a major global center for politics, finance, and culture with a history dating back to Roman times. Governed by the Greater London Authority (GLA) and the Mayor of London, it's known for iconic landmarks like Buckingham Palace, the Tower of London, and the London Eye, and features diverse neighborhoods and world-class attractions.

Key Facts

Location Southeast England, on the River Thames.
Population Around 9 million
Government Greater London Authority
History Founded by Romans as Londinium
